Procedure to borrow book from library:

  1. Books will be issued for 15 days only.
  2. Library Card is mandatory to get the book issued.
  3. Condition of book should be up to the mark or fine will be taken.
  4. Contact the librarian to get the book issued.
  5. Books will only be issued if they are available in stockl
  6. Late fees of Rs. 15 per day will be charged.
